_*Narnia Solo Adventures Commands*_

`![number]` Read page [number]. e.g. `!42`
`!info` Show character stats and inventory.
`!test [stat]` Roll two dice and add your bonus for [stat]. e.g. `!test trick`
`!get [item]` or `!lose [item]` Adds or removes [item] to your inventory. e.g. `!get 3 gold` or `!lose Ring`
`![stat] +[number]` Add [number] to your bonus for stat. e.g. `!fight +1` If `+` omitted, sets stat instead.
`![stat] -[number]` Subtract [number] to your bonus for stat. e.g. `!talk -2`
`!newgame` Rolls and new character and resets the game.
