*Basic Commands*

`!<number>` _Read page <number>. e.g. !42_ `!look` _Re-read last page._
`!info` _Show character stats and inventory. `!s` just shows stats._
`!test luck` or `!test skill` or `!test stam` _Roll a stat test._
`!get <item>` or `!lose <item>` _Adds or removes <item> to your inventory. e.g. `!get 3 gold` or `!lose Ring`_
`!eat` _Eats one provision for 4 stamina._
`!fight <skill> <stamina>` _Fight a monster with skill <skill> and stamina <stamina>. e.g. `!fight 6 2`_
`!<stat> <+/-><amount>` _Set or alter the <stat> with <amount>. e.g. `!stam 3` or `!luck +2`_
`!newgame` _Rolls and new character and resets the game._

`!helpmore` _Sends a DM with all the commands._
